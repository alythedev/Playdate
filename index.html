What is this?

You (and a friend) need to make a game intended to be played by 2 people. Everything else is up to you.


A few examples of what you could make:

  • A local 2-player game like 'Fire Boy Water Girl'.
  • A local co-op split-screen game.
  • A 2-person mystery visual novel where blah idk

How do I participate?

1. Find a "playdate"

Find a friend to make a game with you! If they're not already in the Hack Club Slack, you can invite them yourself.

2. Post your idea in #playdate

Introduce your game idea, who you're working with, etc.

3. Code/draw

You and your "playdate" should track your time seperately. You should both be using Hackatime (for time spent coding) and Lapse (for time spent on art). Make sure both are set up before you start.

4. Share progress

Post detailed updates to your original thread every ~5 hours of work.

5. Publish & get prizes!

Publish your game on Itch.io and submit the form to get your prizes!
